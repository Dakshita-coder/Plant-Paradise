Organic Compost

A nutrient-rich natural fertilizer made from decomposed plant and kitchen waste. Improves soil structure and promotes healthy root growth.

  • Type: Organic Fertilizer
  • Best For: All indoor & outdoor plants
  • Application: Mix with soil before planting or add monthly around plant base
  • Benefits: Enhances moisture retention & soil fertility

Vermicompost

Produced using earthworms, vermicompost boosts plant immunity and accelerates growth by enriching soil with essential nutrients.

  • Type: Organic Fertilizer
  • Best For: Flowering & vegetable plants
  • Application: Use every 20â€“25 days as top dressing
  • Benefits: Increases nitrogen & phosphorus levels

Cocopeat

A lightweight, moisture-retaining growing medium made from coconut husk. Ideal for improving soil aeration and drainage.

  • Type: Soil Conditioner
  • Best For: Indoor plants, seedlings, and pots
  • Application: Mix 30â€“40% cocopeat with garden soil
  • Benefits: Retains water & prevents soil compaction

Red Soil

Rich in iron and minerals, red soil provides strong anchorage for roots and is perfect for outdoor flowering and fruit plants.

  • Type: Natural Soil
  • Best For: Outdoor & flowering plants
  • Application: Use in 50:50 ratio with compost
  • Benefits: Promotes healthy blooming & fruiting
