Bougainvillea

A vibrant, drought-tolerant plant that grows well in full sunlight. Its colorful bracts make your outdoor space lively and tropical.

  • Sowing Time: Spring
  • Best Season: Summer
  • Watering: Twice a week
  • Light: Full sunlight

Money Plant (Pothos)

A fast-growing plant symbolizing prosperity and positivity. It can be grown in pots or hanging baskets outdoors.

  • Sowing Time: Early monsoon
  • Best Season: All seasons
  • Watering: Twice a week
  • Light: Indirect sunlight or partial shade

Marigold (Genda)

A cheerful, easy-to-grow flower plant used widely in Indian gardens and festivals. Its bright blooms last for months.

  • Sowing Time: Summer or early monsoon
  • Best Season: All year round
  • Watering: Regular watering to keep soil moist
  • Light: Full sunlight
